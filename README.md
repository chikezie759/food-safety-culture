# Food Safety Culture Transformation

A comprehensive smart contract system built on Stacks blockchain for transforming food safety culture in organizations through assessment, training, and continuous improvement programs.

## Overview

This project implements a decentralized solution for:

### 🎯 Core Features

- **Safety Culture Assessment**: Comprehensive evaluation system for organizational food safety culture
- **Training Program Management**: Development and tracking of food safety awareness programs  
- **Performance Metrics**: Data-driven insights and improvement tracking
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Automated compliance tracking and reporting
- **Reward System**: Incentive mechanisms for safety culture improvements

### 🏗️ System Architecture

The system consists of three main smart contracts:

1. **Safety Assessment Contract** - Culture evaluation and scoring system
2. **Training Management Contract** - Training program development and tracking
3. **Performance Analytics Contract** - Metrics calculation and reporting

### 🔧 Technology Stack

- **Blockchain**: Stacks (Bitcoin Layer 2)
- **Smart Contracts**: Clarity Language
- **Development Framework**: Clarinet
- **Testing**: Clarinet Test Suite

### 🚀 Key Benefits

- **Transparency**: All assessments and improvements are recorded on-chain
- **Accountability**: Immutable record of safety culture initiatives
- **Incentivization**: Token-based rewards for culture improvements
- **Data-Driven**: Analytics-based decision making for safety programs
- **Scalability**: Supports organizations of all sizes

### 📊 Use Cases

- Food manufacturing facilities
- Restaurant chains and food service
- Agricultural operations
- Food distribution centers
- Regulatory compliance organizations

### 🎯 Target Impact

Transform organizational food safety culture through:
- Measurable culture assessments
- Targeted training interventions
- Continuous improvement cycles
- Performance-based incentives
- Industry-wide best practices sharing
